About Marbella

Marbella was once a small fishing village and it has grown to be one of the most cosmopolitan resorts in the Costa del Sol. The region attracts tourists from all over the world such is the beauty and climate of the place.
The beaches stretch more than thirty kilometres from Guadalmina to Las Champs and have a range of activities such as bars, restaurants and water sports to keep everyone entertained no matter what your interest.
Flight Prices average €100 per person.

Things to do

  • Golf - Marbella is famous for its excellent golf courses

  • Ski

  • Parasailing

  • Go to the WTA Tennis competition held annually

  • For sightseeing go to Marbella Town and head for Plaza De Los Naranjos to get info of the region.

  • Kite-Surfing in Tarifa
