About Barcelona

Home to one of the best football clubs in the world, Barcelona is a mix of different cultures, lively nightlife and art.
Visitors can wander through the streets of the city and experience the art and culture of the city firsthand , relax on a boat in the sunny harbor, or cheer on their favorite sports teams in one of the city's many world-class stadiums.
Flight Prices average €100 per person.

Things to do

  • Watch the football at Nou Camp

  • Visit the Basilica of the Sagrada Familia

  • Explore Gaudi and modernism

  • Experience the local culture and food
