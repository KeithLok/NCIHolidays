About Lanzarote

Situated in the Canary Islands, Lanzarote is the perfect affordable holiday for a Summer break for all ages.
There are camel rides on volcanoes (at Timanfaya National Park) restaurants in a volcanic caves (at Jameos del Agua) and some of the best beaches in the world.
Flight Prices average €100 per person.

Things to do

  • Go Karting at San Bartolome

  • Visit Timanfaya National Park

  • Atlantis Diving Lanzarote at Puerto Del Carmen

  • Whale and Dolphin spotting on a boat ride
