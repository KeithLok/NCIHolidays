About Paris

Known as the City of Lights there is always something going on in Paris. Paris offers the largest concentration of tourist attractions in France, and possibly in Europe. Besides some of the world's most famous musuems, its has a vibrant historic city centre, a beautiful riverscape, an extensive range of historic monuments, including cathedrals, chapels and palaces, plus one of the most famous nightlife scenes in the world. Paris is the city of culture and you are bound to find endless theatres, cinemas and music venues.
Flight Prices average €100 per person.
