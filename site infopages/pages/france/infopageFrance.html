About France

France has been the world's most popular tourist destination for over twenty years and it's one of the most diverse countries in Europe. France contain some of the greatest treasures in Europe, its countryside is prosperous and well tended and it boasts dozens of major tourist attractions, like Paris, the French Riviera, the Atlantic beaches and the winter sport resorts of the French Alps.

Paris

Located in the north of the country on the river Seine, Paris has the reputation of being one of the most beautiful cities in the world, it contains some of the best art, fashion, food and design in the world.

Chamonix

Chamonix is a famous resort in the French Alps at the foot of Mont Blanc. It is also credited with hosting the First Winter Olympic games in 1924. A pefect place for that skii holiday !
