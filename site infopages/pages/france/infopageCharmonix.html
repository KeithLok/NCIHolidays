About Chamonix

Chamonix is the perfect place to come for a weekend break, a week's holiday or a season as you will never tire of things to do.
Chamonix main attractions are Mont-Blanc and the many ski areas that face the Chamonix Valley. Steep slopes and extreme weather conditions suit advanced skiers best, but there are also runs for beginners.
There are plenty of activities including Sports centre, indoor pool with waterslides, paragliding, sleigh rides, ice skating rink, Alpine flights, snow shoeing, cinema and casino.
Flight Prices average €100 per person.
