About Florida

Florida is the most south-eastern state in the United States of America.
It is known as "The Sunshine State" for a good reason with temperatures reaching 32C in Summer.
The capital of Florida is Tallahassee. The beaches are among the most popular attractions, along with some of the world's best known theme parks, including Disney World, Universal Studios, Busch Gardens and SeaWorld. Orlando attracts more visitors than any other amusement park destination in the United States. However some of Florida's best-kept secrets are in secluded locations away from tourist areas, but well worth seeing. Florida has something to offer for any kind of traveler whether you want a beach break in Miami or family holiday at Disney World !
Flight Prices average €540 per person.

Things to do

  • Disney World - located in Orlando
  • Disney's Hollywood Studios
  • SeaWorld Adventure Park Orlando
  • Discovery Cove
  • See the John F. Kennedy Space Center
  • Go to the numerous beaches with Panama City Beach, Daytona Beach, South Beach, and West Palm Beach being some of the best in the state.
