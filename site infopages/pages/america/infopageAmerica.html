About The US

The United States of America is the perfect place to Holiday. There is so much to do including visiting the sights in New York, trying your luck in Las Vegas or taking the kids to Disneyworld in Orlando !

New York

New York City is a center for media, culture, food, fashion, art, research, finance, and trade. It has one of the largest and most famous skylines on earth, dominated by the iconic Empire State Building. A great place for a city break all year round.

Florida

Florida is the perfect place for a holiday with the family with so much to do. With 87.3 million visitors in 2011, Florida is the top travel destination in the world.

Las Vegas

Known as the Entertainment Capital of the World, there is always something to do in Vegas. Whether you are into gambling, music or plays.. or a quick marriage! You will never be short of things to do.
