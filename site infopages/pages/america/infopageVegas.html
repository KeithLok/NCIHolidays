About Las Vegas

Situated in the middle of the Nevada desert, Las Vegas is within day-trip distance to some of America's most visited natural sites.
Las Vegas has 18 out of 21 of the largest hotels in the world and walking down 'The Strip' visitors will see the skylines of New York and Paris, discover the canals of Venice and the Pyramids of Egypt. Vegas is the Entertainment Capital of the world and there is always something to do such as Gambling, shows or sight-seeing.
Not only is Vegas great for a carefree break it has also become a place for families to enjoy a break together in family resorts and tours.
Flight Prices average €540 per person.

Things to do

  • The top of the Stratosphere Tower - at 1,149 feet you can see all of Las Vegas and its attractions.
  • The Bellagio Gallery of Fine Art
  • Madame Tussaud's Wax Museum
  • Acts at Caesars Palace hotel
  • The Beatles - Love - Cirque du Soleil
