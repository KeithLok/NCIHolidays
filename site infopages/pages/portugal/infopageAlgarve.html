About The Algarve

The Algarve is Portugal's most popular holiday destination due to the 200km of clean beaches, the cool and unpolluted water, very safe, affordable and overall welcoming. English is spoken at most of the resorts. Algarve is rich in culture and diversity. If you are looking for fast paced resorts or a calm tranquil setting both is attainable here at Algarve.

Things to do

  • Golfing at the Castro Marim Golfe and Country Club
  • Relax at the many beaches available
  • Relax in the Spa in the mount of Monchique
  • Admire the rock formations scattered along the coastlines
