About Lisbon

Lisbon enjoys a warm climate, with mild winters and very warm summers. Strongly influenced by the Gulf Stream it is one of the mildest climates in Europe. The city stretches along the northern bank of the river Tejo as it flows into the Atlantic Ocean.

Things to do

  • Cine Theatro Gymnasium
  • Picnic in Jardim Botanico
  • Explore lush gardens of the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation
  • Go clubbing
