About Portugal

Portugal is one of the warmest European countries. In mainland Portugal, yearly temperature averages are about 15C in the North and 18C in the South. Being 900 years old, it holds many historical sites for tourists to see.

Lisbon

The capital of Portugal, Lisbon has experienced a renaissance in recent years, with a contemporary culture that is alive and thriving and making its mark in today's Europe.

The Algarve

The Algarve is the southernmost region of Portugal, on the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. It has many beaches and rock formations along the coast.

Maderia

Madeira is a sub-tropical archipelago located in the Atlantic Ocean and an autonomous region of Portugal.
