About Madeira

Madeira Islands are just a short trip from Europe; more or less than 4 hours from the United Kingdom. Madeira is a holiday destination where you can combine holidays by the sea, in the mountains or in the city all into one. Nowadays Madeira is a popular destination for tourists of all ages. Its constant mild climate keeps the spring on Madeira all year round.
