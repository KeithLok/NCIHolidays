About Venice

Venice is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It has stunning architecture. Mysterious passageways. And of course, the canals. This sanctuary on a lagoon is virtually the same as it was six hundred years ago, which adds to the fascinating character. Venice has decayed since its heyday and is heavily touristed (there are slightly more tourists than residents), but the romantic charm remains.

Things to do

  • Ride on a Gondola
  • Spend a day on the islands, mainly Murano, Burano and Torcello
  • Visit the Riviera old canals - del Brenta
